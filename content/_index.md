## Welcome to our Dialogue.

We're a group of Northeastern University students living and studying in Belgium this summer as part of a Dialogue of Civilizations program. Our focus is the intersection of **data science and international politics** — how information flows, how institutions decide, and how the two shape each other on this side of the Atlantic.

From Brussels to Leuven, we're meeting with policymakers, exploring EU institutions, and digging into the data behind the headlines. This blog is where we'll share what we're learning, what we're building, and a bit of what life looks like along the way.

\- Alyssa, Bina, Charlie, and Tyler
